Team Member Terms Of Use.

1. Terms

By being part of our team, you should respect the following rules:

    - Staying calm during arguments and or other manners.
    - Do not steal code from other team members or other mods without OE-O or the mod creator/developers permissions.
    - Do not harrass or discriminate other team members.
    - Do not send OE-O Source Code without permission to non-team members.

Breakage of these rules will follow in a warning, ban or removal from OE-O indefinitely.
